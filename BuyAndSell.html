Objetives


This innovative app redefines the buying and selling experience for video games and movies within a secure and user-friendly platform. Built on ServiceNow, it offers a vast selection of entertainment options, facilitating seamless transactions and promoting a vibrant community of enthusiasts. Features include an intuitive interface, secure payment systems, peer reviews, personalized recommendations, and easy listing tools for sellers. Designed to integrate effortlessly into the ServiceNow ecosystem, this marketplace not only supports sustainable entertainment practices by giving games and movies a second life but also provides an engaging platform for users to connect and share their passions.

Technologies

-ServiceNow: as Aplication as a Service

-ServiceNow Framework all the tools that ServiceNow provides to customize the user Experience like Business Rules, Service Catalog, UI Policies....
